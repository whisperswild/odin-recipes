Rib Rub

A rib rub for ribs

This delicious rib rub is a delicious way to add rub to ribs!

    Ingredients

  • Brown Sugar
  • White Sugar
  • Paprika
  • Garlic Powder
  • Ground Black Pepper
  • Ground Ginger Powder
  • Onion Powder
  • Rosemary Powder

    Directions

  1. Mix all ingredients together in a bowl.
  2. Rub rib rub on to ribs
